Skyblock Tycoon

A single-player map designed for those player's who seek both relaxation and adventure from the comfort of their brick house amidst a vibrant, green expanse. With an average gameplay duration of 3.2 hours and a remarkable player retention rate of 72%, this game promises an engaging and satisfying experience.

World One: The Overworld

The tycoon adventure begins in the Overworld, where lush grasslands and towering trees beckon. Here, the foundation of the player's empire awaits, featuring a quaint brick house, a bountiful wheat farm, and a rich iron mine. Progressing through the game to unlock the late-game factory, amplifying their production and success.

World Two: The Moon

Take a giant leap to the Moon, where low gravity and luminous crystals create a stark contrast to the Overworld. This celestial domain offers a fresh challenge with its focus on mining for precious crystals, replacing the gold rush of the first world. Player's can explore multiple moon platforms, each offering unique items for purchase, including rockets, drills, and advanced factories, to propel their tycoon journey to new heights.

Project Main Features

  • Two Unique Worlds: Transition seamlessly from the green vistas of the Overworld to the mystic expanse of the Moon, each world offering distinct resources, challenges, and opportunities.
  • Engaging Gameplay: With an intuitive design focused on relaxation and progression, immerse yourself in a gameplay experience that keeps you coming back for more.
