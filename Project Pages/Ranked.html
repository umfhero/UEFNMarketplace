Small low memory-cost map

Ranked 1v1 is designed for players who seek a platform to test their skill against equally skilled opponents. It's a map where skills are honed through a ranked system visually shown on screen, tactics are evolved, and players are pushed to evolve theire skills. With state-of-the-art features like auto respawn, a ranked system through verse, and the saving of inventories and ranks.

Project Main Features

  • Map Reset: To maintain fairness and the element of surprise, the map resets, ensuring every duel offers a fresh start, challenging players to adapt and strategize anew.
  • Persistent Inventories and Rankings: players arsenal and accomplishments follow them. Inventories and rank positions are saved, reflecting your growth and skill over time, giving every match significance in their journey to dominate.
  • Ranked System: Engage in the thrill of competition with a comprehensive ranked system. Climb the ranks through each pre set visual going through bronze to unreal.
  • Instant Respawn: Keeping the action non-stop, the map features an automatic respawn system that puts players back into the game immediately after being taken down, ensuring the battle's pace never stops.
